AR Gym

AR Gym is a mobile application for gym beginners who are unsure of how to use gym equipments. The application allows the user to scan a image on a gym equipment and view a 3D male/female model performing the exercises on a gym equipment. This allows the users to understand how the equipment is used as well as the right posture that they should have while working out. The application also provides routines for all levels of gym users (Beginner, Advanced, Expert) and video demonstrations of exercises.

It was developed as my Final Year Project in University, using C# in Unity3D and the Vuforia Engine, and the models were done and animated in Blender.
